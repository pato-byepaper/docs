# Procedimiento Operativo Estándar para el Puntaje de Salud del Hotel

Este SOP describe cómo se calcula, interpreta y mejora el **Puntaje de Salud del Hotel**, un indicador integral de mantenimiento, satisfacción del huésped y eficiencia operativa.

<Note>
Antes de iniciar, asegúrate de tener acceso al módulo de mantenimiento, reportes e inspecciones del sistema del hotel.
</Note>
---
### Initial Setup and Baseline Health

When a hotel is first set up in the system, it begins with a baseline health score of **85%**. This starting point accounts for:

- **No Inspection History**: The system assumes proper initial conditions
- **No Maintenance Backlog**: No existing maintenance issues are assumed
- **No Active Reports**: The system starts with a clean slate for reporting

**Important Notes:**
- This baseline score will adjust as actual data is collected
- The score may decrease if initial inspections reveal issues
- Proactive maintenance and timely issue resolution can help maintain or improve the score
- The system will generate its first set of maintenance schedules and inspection cycles based on this baseline

The baseline of 85% represents a "Good" starting point, encouraging property managers to:
1. Conduct initial inspections promptly
2. Address any immediate issues
3. Establish regular maintenance routines
4. Build a history of property conditions

## Health Calculation by Level

### 1. Element Level
**Calculation:**
- Base score: 100 points
- Penalties applied based on:
  - Element condition (e.g., "fuera de servicio" = -40, "requiere mantenimiento" = -25)
  - Active reports (critical issues = -30, others = -15)
  - Checklist failures (base penalty = 20 per failure)
  - Maintenance overdue (-2 points per day overdue)

**Impacting Factors:**
- Element status/condition
- Associated reports and their priority
- Maintenance history and due dates
- Checklist item results and follow-up status

### 2. Space Level
**Calculation:**
- Weighted average of all elements in the space (30% weight)
- Maintenance score based on inspection recency (25% weight)
- Report penalties (35% weight)
- Checklist impact (10% weight)

**Impacting Factors:**
- Number and severity of active reports
- Inspection status and recency
- Element health scores within the space
- Checklist compliance
- Follow-up item resolution time

### 3. Floor Level
**Calculation:**
- Average of all space health scores on the floor
- Additional consideration for floor-specific issues
- Weighted by space importance/function

**Impacting Factors:**
- Health of individual spaces
- Common area conditions
- Floor-specific maintenance needs
- Traffic/usage patterns

### 4. Building Level
**Calculation:**
- Weighted average of all floors in the building
- Building-wide systems impact (HVAC, electrical, plumbing)
- Common area conditions

**Impacting Factors:**
- Floor health scores
- Building systems status
- Shared facility conditions
- Building age and maintenance history

### 5. Hotel Level
**Calculation Methods:**
1. **Space-Weighted (Default):** 
   - Calculates health for each space
   - Averages all space health scores
   - Best for properties with uniform space importance

2. **Floor-Weighted:**
   - Aggregates spaces by floor first
   - Averages floor health scores
   - Best for multi-building properties

**Impacting Factors:**
- Overall maintenance backlog
- Critical infrastructure status
- Guest-impacting issues
- Inspection compliance
- Staff responsiveness to issues
- Historical maintenance patterns

### Cross-Level Considerations:
- **Critical Issues:** Have cascading impact across multiple levels
- **Common Areas:** Affect both their specific level and the overall property
- **Guest-Facing Areas:** May be weighted more heavily in calculations
- **High-Traffic Areas:** May have more frequent inspections and stricter standards

## Notas de Precaución

<Warning>
Resuelve primero reportes críticos y fallas con más de 30 días, ya que generan la mayor penalización.
</Warning>

<Warning>
Documenta con fotos cada falla para mejorar trazabilidad y control del historial de mantenimiento.
</Warning>

---

## Consejos de Eficiencia

<Tip>
Capacita a tu equipo en inspecciones sistemáticas para reducir el número de fallas con seguimiento abierto.
</Tip>

<Tip>
Monitorea la diferencia entre puntaje actual y proyectado cada semana para anticipar problemas.
</Tip>

<Tip>
Utiliza el sistema de alertas como una lista de prioridades para asignar recursos de manera eficiente.
</Tip>
